What We Do

Complete Auto Services

End-to-end automotive solutions for Ghanaians — from sourcing to delivery.

Car Importation

We source and import premium vehicles directly from USA via Copart and IAAI auction platforms — handling everything from bidding to delivery in Ghana.

Clearing Agent Services

Full port clearance management. We calculate exact import duties upfront so you know total costs before committing — no surprises at the port.

Installment Plans

Flexible payment plans tailored to your budget. Buy premium vehicles without paying the full amount upfront. Terms structured to suit your financial situation.

Our Story

Ghana's Trusted Auto Link

VicDavis Auto Link was founded with a single mission: give Ghanaians access to premium quality vehicles at fair prices. As official partners of Copart and IAAI — America's largest auto auction platforms — we bring the USA car market directly to your doorstep in Accra.
